Movement is My Voice

Exploring life through rhythm and dance

Personal Background

I have always loved dancing. From childhood through secondary school, Chinese dance was the only genre I practiced. After graduating from secondary school, I began to fully explore my passion for dance.

I specifically went to various dance studios to learn different styles, including street dance, choreography, jazz, K-pop, hip-hop, and jazz funk. From 2024 up to the present, I have had the opportunity to participate in various types of performances and video shoots. Through these diverse opportunities, I have been able to explore different sides of myself.
